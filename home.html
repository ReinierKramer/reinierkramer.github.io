--- --- Reinier Kramer

I am assistant professor (ricercatore a tempo determinato tenure track) at the Università degli Studi di Milano-Bicocca, in the mathematical physics group of the Dipartimento di Matematica e Applicazioni.

I finished my PhD in geometry and mathematical physics at the University of Amsterdam under the supervision of Sergey Shadrin in June 2019.
From October 2019 to September 2021, I did a postdoc at the Max Planck Institute for Mathematics in Bonn, working with Gaëtan Borot. There, I also organised a reading group on intersection theory on stacks.
From October 2021 to August 2024, I was a postdoc at the University of Alberta in the group of Vincent Bouchard. From October 2021 to September 2023, I was partially supported by a PIMS postdoctoral fellowship.
